<p><p>The rapid diagnostics market is divided into two main types: Over-the-Counter (OTC) rapid diagnostics tests and professional rapid diagnostics tests. OTC tests are designed for consumer use, allowing individuals to perform tests at home for various conditions, such as pregnancy or infections, without medical supervision. In contrast, professional rapid diagnostics tests are conducted by healthcare providers in clinical settings, offering more complex analysis and often requiring specialized equipment. Both types provide quick results, enhancing timely decision-making in healthcare.</p></p>
