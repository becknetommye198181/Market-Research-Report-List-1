<p><p>Doppler Ultrasound Systems are categorized into handheld and trolley-based systems. Handheld Doppler systems are portable devices that offer convenience for quick assessments in various clinical settings, allowing for easy mobility and point-of-care diagnostics. In contrast, trolley-based Doppler systems are larger, stationary setups typically found in hospitals, providing advanced imaging capabilities and greater functionality for in-depth examinations. Both types serve essential roles in monitoring blood flow and detecting vascular conditions, catering to different clinical needs and environments.</p></p>
